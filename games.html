Games

  • Interstellar Escape

    Made with Games For Love where I collaborated with a team of interns to create a story based collection of arcade games. The player is stranded on an undiscovered planet, and is trying to escape. They must face challenges put forth by he planet's inhabitants, in a brick breaker style mini game. Tiles have different effects such as slowing down the ball, shrinking or enlargening the player, and more. I implemented and wrote the story, designed the menus and UI, and performed bug fixes. This was made in Unity using C#. There are multiple levels with different gameplay mechanics, and that augment the story with player interaction.

  • Escape the Kitchen

    Made with Games For Love where I collaborated with a team of interns to create a 2-D platformer where the player plays as a chicken drumstick, and is trying to escape the kitchen. It was made in Unity using C#, and I implemented UI design, menus, and player interactability. There are 3 levels, with each one introducing a new mechanic. I worked in both script writing and design.
